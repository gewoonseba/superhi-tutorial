Aria is an independent digital artist and puzzle enthusiast whose work intersects geometric abstraction and portraiture. She is based in Portland, Oregon.

Lost and found

"Lost and Found" is a compelling project featured in my portfolio that delves into the theme of rediscovery amidst chaos. Through a series of multimedia installations, the project juxtaposes items and memories presumed lost with their unexpected reappearances, exploring the emotional resonance of finding what was once thought to be gone forever. This work aims to evoke a sense of nostalgia and reflection, encouraging viewers to contemplate the transient nature of loss and the joy of unexpected recoveries.

You Know Me and You Don't

"You Know Me and You Don't" is an intriguing project in my portfolio that explores the complexities of identity and perception. Through a blend of interactive art pieces and digital media, this work challenges viewers to reconsider their assumptions and understandings of the familiar. It delves into the multifaceted nature of individual personas, revealing hidden layers and unexpected facets that prompt a deeper inquiry into how we perceive ourselves and others. This project invites an introspective journey, highlighting the fluidity and depth of human identity.

Far Away

Exploring themes of distance and longing, "Far Away" is a poignant project that delves into the emotional landscapes of separation and connection. This work uses a blend of photography and narrative to convey the sense of yearning and hope that accompanies being far from loved ones or familiar places. Through its evocative imagery and heartfelt stories, the project captures the delicate balance between the sorrow of being apart and the beauty of cherished memories and anticipated reunions. "Far Away" invites viewers to reflect on their own experiences of distance and the powerful bonds that endure despite it.

Fierce

A celebration of strength and resilience, "Fierce" stands out in my portfolio for its powerful portrayal of tenacity in the face of adversity. This project combines striking visuals with raw, emotive storytelling to highlight the inner strength of individuals overcoming significant challenges. Through a series of compelling photographs and narratives, "Fierce" captures the essence of determination and the indomitable human spirit, inspiring viewers to reflect on their own inner reserves of courage and perseverance.
